What we offer

MojaCard

MojaCard enables anyone to create a virtual prepaid card that can be preloaded and used across the globe where traditional debit/credit cards are accepted. MojaCard users can instantly load their cards using Mobile Money wallets or Bank Accounts, enabling them to shop online in stores like ebay, alibaba, amazon etc. or paying for services like education, health and many more.

MojaRemit

Forget all the hustle of excessive money remittance charges! MojaRemit will benefit from the exchange rates rather than levy charges for Moja users to send and receive money to their loved ones.

MojaSure

Business continuity has always been a challenge and more so for SMEs as they do not have the financial muscle to cover some of their unforeseen challenges. MojaSure makes it possible for individuals and SMEs to insure what is dear to them ensuring that business can continue even when disaster has struck.
